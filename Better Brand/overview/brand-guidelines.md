## Voice & Tone

### We Sound Like:
- Confident, not arrogant
- Direct, not blunt
- Creative, not gimmicky
- Professional, not corporate
- Human, not robotic

### We Never Sound Like:
- Generic marketing speak ("synergy," "leverage," "disrupt")
- Overly casual or slangy
- Desperate or salesy
- Vague or wishy-washy

### Key Phrases We Use:
- "Your website should work as hard as you do"
- "Design that converts" / "Design that performs"
- "Built for results, not just looks"
- Specifics over superlatives — show, don't tell

### Key Things We Avoid:
- "We use AI to..." — capability speaks for itself
- "Affordable" / "cheap" — we're premium
- "One-size-fits-all" — everything is custom
- Over-promising timelines or results

## Content Rules
1. Every piece of content should demonstrate skill, not just claim it
2. Show the work — process shots, before/afters, real results
3. Keep it tight — no filler, no fluff
4. One clear message per post
5. Always include a reason for the viewer to care
