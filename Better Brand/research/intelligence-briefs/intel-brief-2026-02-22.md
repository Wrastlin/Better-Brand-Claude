## Competitor Landscape: AI Web Design

### Top Enterprise Players (2026)
Source: Superside, Blend B2B, and industry roundups

The market is splitting into two tiers:
1. **Enterprise agencies** ($10K-$100K+ projects) — Superside, Blend B2B, established agencies adding AI
2. **AI website builders** (DIY tools) — Wix AI, Framer, Webflow, PlayCode, SuperNinja

### The Gap BBD Should Own
Between expensive agencies and cheap DIY tools, there's a massive underserved middle:
- **Small businesses who can't afford $10K+ agencies**
- **Businesses who tried DIY and got generic results**
- **Local businesses who need someone to just handle it**

BBD's sweet spot: **$2K-$10K custom websites** that look like they cost $20K, delivered faster than traditional agencies because of AI-powered workflow.

### AI Website Builders (What We're Competing Against)
| Tool | Price | Strength | Weakness |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Framer | Free-$25/mo | Beautiful templates | Still requires design eye |
| Wix AI | Free-$17/mo | Easy | Generic output |
| Webflow | $14-$39/mo | Powerful | Complex learning curve |
| PlayCode | Various | AI-driven | New, unproven |
| SuperNinja | Free-$16/mo | Full AI generation | Limited customization |

**Our positioning against these:** "AI tools build websites. We build businesses." The tool outputs generic. We output custom, strategic, conversion-focused.
